For more than a century, the YWCA World Service Council has supported the World YWCA in its outreach to more than 100 YWCAs around the globe.

The World Service Council is the primary financial supporter of the young women’s training programs of the World YWCA, and additionally assists the World YWCA’s work with member YWCAs that strengthen services and activities. A special fund helps the World YWCA General Secretary respond to emergency situations that may arise from climate disasters, conflict and more recently, a pandemic. Each year, the World YWCA submits a budget request to the World Service Council with a goal that the World Service Council works hard to meet, We appreciate your help in our efforts to make the world a better place for women and girls. Thank you for giving what you can.

YWCA USA is on a mission to eliminate racism, empower women, stand up for social justice, help families, and strengthen communities. For over 160 years we’ve been at the forefront of the most pressing social movements — from voting rights to civil rights, from affordable housing to pay equity, from violence prevention to health care reform. We combine programming and advocacy to generate institutional change in three key areas: racial justice and civil rights, empowerment and economic advancement, and health and safety of women and girls.

Thank you for giving what you can! Your donation to YWCA USA will strengthen our support for associations around the country and fund robust advocacy efforts to educate and partner with lawmakers on policies that advance our critical mission of eliminating racism and empowering women.
